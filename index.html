About

The K.I.D.S (Knee Injury Detection System) is a cutting-edge innovation crafted by Deependra and Sairam, students from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Chennai. Functioning as an intelligent radiology assistant, K.I.D.S goes beyond conventional diagnostics by proficiently identifying knee injuries and providing a detailed grading of their severity. This groundbreaking system utilizes advanced algorithms to not only detect but also categorize knee injuries, offering valuable insights for medical practitioners.

Moreover, K.I.D.S showcases its prowess by extending its diagnostic capabilities to the identification of three distinct diseases namely, ACL tears, Meniscal tears and Osteoarthritis marking a significant stride in the realm of medical technology and enhancing the efficiency of healthcare systems.

Organization

Larsen & Toubro (L&T) is a prominent multinational conglomerate based in India, renowned for its leadership in engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) services. Established in 1938 by Holck-Larsen and Toubro, the company has significantly influenced India's infrastructure development. Operating across diverse sectors like engineering, construction, information technology, financial services, and manufacturing, L&T has successfully executed a wide array of projects both domestically and internationally. Notable for its commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability, L&T has left its mark on various iconic infrastructure projects, including bridges, highways, airports, and power plants.

The company's expertise extends to heavy engineering, defense, aerospace, and technology services. L&T's emphasis on responsible business practices and corporate social responsibility is reflected in its dedication to community development, ' education, healthcare, and environmental conservation. Known for delivering complex projects with precision and adhering to the highest safety standards, L&T stands as a key player in the global engineering and construction industry.
